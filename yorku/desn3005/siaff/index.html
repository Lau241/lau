Structure in our lives comes from a sense of balance.

The Seoul International Architecture Film Festival (SIAFF) is an annual festival based in South Korea dedicated to showcasing films across the world about architecture, design, city planning, and urban development.

Every year a new theme is dedicated to the spirit of architecture. This year, the festival will follow the theme of balance. Balance between form and function, structure and creativity, work and life. Is architecture fair, equal, or sustainable? As an industry, we must delve deeper into investigating these balances.
