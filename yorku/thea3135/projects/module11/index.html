Press release outline

- Background on the conference purpose and focus on design education in Canada (connection with other professors, understanding different perspectives on education, responding to changes in the field)

- Overview of key talks and presentation themes (opening/closing keynotes, keynote speakers, topic summary, and overall conference themes)

- Quote from organizer on why the conference matters (relevance of collaboration, urgency of adapting education models to fit the technology advancements in the field)

- Schedule highlights and session formats (list of featured speakers, topics, featured guests)

- Registration details and deadlines, location access, and audience information (who should be attending, how to register, campus location, time schedule)
