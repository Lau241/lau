Welcome to TheatreTO

TheatreTO is a non-profit charitable organization committed to celebrating and sustaining live performance across new and evolving spaces. Expanding beyond its origins, TheatreTO brings its artistic spirit to multiple venues, transforming both traditional and unexpected locations into stages for connection, creativity, and community.
